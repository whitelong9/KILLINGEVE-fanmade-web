Storyline

Eve is a bored, whip-smart security services operative
whose desk-bound job doesn't fulfill her fantasies of being a spy.
Villanelle is a talented killer, who clings to the luxuries her violent
job affords her. These two fierce women, equally obsessed with
each other, will go head to head in an epic game of cat and
mouse, toppling the typical spy-action thriller.
